Group: Ivy, Werner, Jorge

Concept:

We had this idea of creating a web page that displays an assortment of albums for users to view. When the user clicks on one of the album covers, it displays all of its information ranging from who created it, the release date and its length to name a few. It would also play a small snippet once the user clicks on the album cover as well as having the color scheme change to that exact album cover.

Design Process:

Research:

At first, it was a bit difficult to find something that really spoke to us, going through the websites listed on the GitHub Research Sheet and eventually going through other pages like Tumblr & Are.na where we were able to look through certain web page designs and came across a couple that helped inspire us and flesh out our concept from working with music in some way to then developing the concept we have now.

Below are some pages that we used to look up websites that have helped throughout our research, some of these pages helped more than the other although each was vital into making sure our design process worked well enough.

  • https://www.are.na/
  • https://github.com (How To Research: Week 2 pagety)
  • https://www.tumblr.com/themes

Inspiration:

While looking through Are.na we found some interesting web pages that were actually really cool design wise but some that spoke out to us where the ones listed down below. The big inspiration that really got us motivated was the daisy chain studio website which is the first linked website. The way the nav bar works is something that felt new to us and gave us that extra boost of creativity to keep looking for more and more pages to get any sort of inspiration off of.

While looking at these pages we did decide that the color scheme we want to keep is the black and white or shades similar to those to keep that aesthetic up. Although as mentioned earlier once we click on an album cover, the page switches color schemes to reflect based on the album that was clicked.

Below are the pages that we came across during the research aspect of the design process. All of us were inspired by how the layout and how they displayed their info to users.

  • https://www.daisychainstudio.net/
  • https://www.bros.family/work
  • https://letude.group/work

Wireframes:

Down below we have linked is a figma page that has all of our details and how we planned this out. We also have some wireframes to plan out how the page will look like. Some ideas we had was that the nav bar would act as an album selector where they could scroll through a list of albums, click on it and you'll get a set of information based on the album. Another idea we had would be having the nav bar on the left hand side and then a block of albums to go through and when clicking on them would share all of its information.

Figma Wireframes

Technical Approach:

The approach we are going for in this project would be to curate and scrap data into a new dataset that would be handled in JSON format. This is something we haven't learned how to do so it would be a welcoming challenge and also a new skill to learn how to create datasets of our own and use them for projects down the line.
