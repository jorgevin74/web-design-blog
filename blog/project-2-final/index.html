Live Blog Site : Project 2

I was in charge of working on the front end side so dealing with the njk files and such. I decided to begin laying out the different kinds of pages we would need so an njk file for the home or index, another for an about page and it kept on going. Eventually deciding to organize the page with a header.njk and a footer.njk and a nav.njk to use them in any other pages we woulld've created. I also incorporated a couple bits of Javascript into our page as well. I decided to work with Motion.Js and GSAP to bring in a bit of interactivity within the page. I added a hover effect as well as a press effect whenever an album was being clicked on or viewed in the nav bar. I also went ahead and added a text animation as well to give it a bit of dyanmic effect. I then lastly ended up adding a sort of tilt animation when hovering over the album cover. It would look towards the perspective of where the mouse was, although difficult to control I managed to incorporate it into the page. Overall I am pretty satisfied with the work we have done especially doing it with our own database was a nice change of pace.

JS Library Used: Motion.Js GSAP
